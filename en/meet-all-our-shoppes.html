MEET ALL OUR SHOPPES

Drawing on their extensive experience and success in the retail sector, Sands Retail has created an impressive portfolio of dream destinations for the world’s most avid fashion enthusiasts. From the pulsating excitement of Macao’s Cotai Strip to the energetic metropolis of Singapore, a vast and enticing array of luxury boutiques treat visitors to a symphony of sights, sounds and emotions. Here, personal style is an art form.

Sands Retail is the leasing and retail asset management platform of Las Vegas Sands Corp - responsible for properties across all LVSC owned malls in Macao, Singapore and future international developments.
