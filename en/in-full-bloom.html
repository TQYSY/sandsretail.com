DECORATION: Nature’s Majesty


Capture the moment as you enjoy elegant horticultural arrangements on display at Shoppes at Four Seasons, Shoppes at Venetian and Shoppes at Cotai Central, 29 May through 31 July 2014.

Renowned Hong Kong food blogger KC Koo visited the Shoppes and shared an insightful opinion of his dining experiences with us.

"The choices of gastronomic experiences located in the Sands Cotai properties in Macao will give even the most fastidious of eaters ‘food for the soul’!

Enjoy a chilled beer and delicious gastropub food with friends in McSorley's Ale House, or authentic Cantonese cuisine such as abalone in Elegant Seafood Restaurant. Tired of old fashioned Shanghainese cuisine? The innovative menu of 456 Modern Shanghainese Cuisine Restaurant will satisfy those taste buds desperate for a new burst of flavour, with their special twist on traditional dishes such as “Sweet and Sour Pork” and “Sautéed King Prawns”. For a spice hit you can find many classic Thai dishes in Café Siam that will leave you energized and alert from the combination of special Thai seasonings! Those with large appetites should head to the Brazilian Fogo Samba restaurant for an endless supply of freshly carved, juicy meats complimented by delicious Brazilian side dishes. Not a meat eater? No worries, there is always Lei Garden that never fails to disappoint with their sumptuous seafood and award winning dishes.

Those looking for something more atmospheric and different, Edo Japanese Restaurant and Old HK Restaurant are both situated besides the canal for a more scenic meal. Dining here, especially in the outdoor space, watching the world go by, is soothing, relaxing and unique. On the western cuisine front, customers who opt for a more comfortable yet spacious venue can consider Café Deco which serves international buffet with a wide variety of choices, or the renowned Morton's The SteakHouse which serves delicious high-grade top quality beef steaks. Lastly, for a romance dinner for two or a few couples, the Madeira Portuguese Restaurant or Vergnano Italian Restauran has the perfect combination of opulent Mediterranean food, good wine and a soothing atmosphere.

One thing is for sure, these restaurants all have a little something special about them given their unique location in Macao which further adds to the unique experience. My advice to you…. start planning your culinary journey of discovery!"

About KC Koo

KC is one of the most influential food critics in Hong Kong, with one of the highest daily hit rate in town for his food blog (www.gourmetkc.com). Published more than 16,000 food critics in different platforms throughout the past 2 years, he has visited more than 15,000 restaurants not only from Hong Kong and Macao, but also Japan, Singapore, Thailand and even as far as Spain, to name a few. In addition, he is also the published author of three gourmet guide books and a popular columnist of food magazines and newspaper. At the same time he also participates in the production of TV food programs, making him a true 360 multi-platform gourmet!
