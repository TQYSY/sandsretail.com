The Shoppes at Four Seasons exemplifies the distinctive sophistication universally associated with the Four Seasons brand. Nestled inside this majestic hotel, where honoured guests are warmly welcomed and extensively pampered in 360 opulent rooms and suites, with a luxurious full-service spa and thrilling high-end gaming. The Shoppes at Four Seasons surpasses the expectations of even the most discerning guests. Visitors enjoy a sense of privilege and pride as they browse boutiques from over 150 of the world’s most prestigious luxury brands, featuring bespoke apparel with a classic appeal that transcends the ebb and flow of fleeting trends. Personalised services, such as individual assistance from a shopping consultant, are also available. This is the height of luxury retail.

  • Exemplifies the distinctive sophistication universally associated with the Four Seasons brand
  • Over 150 of the world’s most prestigious luxury brands
  • 260,000 square feet of opulent retail space
  • Located inside The Four Seasons Hotel with 360 luxury suites
  • Linked by air-conditioned footbridge to Shoppes at Venetian and Shoppes at Cotai Central
  • High-end boutique gaming
