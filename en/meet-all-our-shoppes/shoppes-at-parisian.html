Paris: The Timeless and Enchanting "City of Lights"

Stunning jewel of the Cotai Strip®, The Parisian brings the delights and charms of one of the world’s favourite travel destinations to Asia. Amidst the iconic sights of the Eiffel Tower and Les Jardins and the tempting aromas of sidewalk cafés, pâtisseries and bistros, Shoppes at Parisian features the latest designs from an exclusive selection of the world’s most celebrated fashion designers, as well as up-and-coming labels that remain elusively ahead of the latest trends. The target is a young, affluent demographic with a keen eye for the cutting edge in fashion and the discretionary income to acquire pieces that reflect their prosperity. The Parisian embodies the timeless poetry and elegance of the legendary City of Lights and sets the scene for an incomparable shopping experience for the new generation of luxury clientele.

  • Brings the delights of one of the world favourite travel destination to Asia
  • The magnificent Eiffel Tower
  • Over 300,000 square feet of retail space
  • Over 170 boutiques by precincts of Paris
  • 3,000 hotel suites and over 15 restaurants
  • Linked by air-conditioned footbridge to Shoppes at Four Seasons
