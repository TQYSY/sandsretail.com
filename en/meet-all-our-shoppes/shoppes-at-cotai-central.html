Natural Oasis in a Family Haven

Shoppes at Cotai is a beautiful place to spend an afternoon with family and friends, offering a peaceful sanctuary in the heart of a high-energy integrated resort city: Sands Cotai Central. Just steps away from the largest Conrad, Sheraton and Holiday Inn properties in the world, the thrill and excitement of gaming, a tantalising selection of fine restaurants – all under one roof – its premium location makes it an ideal shopping destination for business and leisure travellers. For the children, a sensational new DreamWorks Experience at Cotai Strip™ Resorts gives young fans the chance to interact with their favourite animated characters. Amidst the excitement, shoppers enter a bastion of serenity, where abundant sunlight, a three-storey cascading waterfall, rocky cliff faces and lush plant life create an inviting environment for over 180 shops, featuring an attractive family mix of international brands. . Here, guests enjoy an unparalleled luxury duty-free shopping experience that is singularly enhanced by the splendour of the natural world.
